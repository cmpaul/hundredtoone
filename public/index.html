How does it work?

Click 'Start!' and write down 100 ideas. A timer will encourage you to write quickly, allowing you to bypass critical evaluation and tap into the creative power of your subconscious mind.

🤔✍📝

When you're done, review and prioritize your list. Explore your top 10 ideas. Make a plan to take critical action on one. That's how you go from 💯 to one!

💯🔟🥇

To improve your follow through, opt in to receive regular email reminders of your action plan. Share your plan with others to stay accountable.
